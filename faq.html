F.A.Q. (Frequently Asked Questions)

Where are you located?

We are in North Austin near the Research Blvd. (Hwy-183) and Hwy-620/I-45 interchange, offering easy access from Research Blvd. Our office address is 13740 Research Blvd., P-4, Austin, TX 78750. We're on the southbound Research Blvd. frontage road just south of Lake Creek Pkwy. in a building complex named Lake Creek Office Park. For more detailed directions and a map, please see our Location page.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept personal checks and cash. We cannot accept credit or debit cards.

What are your office hours?

The office is open Monday to Thursday from 8:00am - 12:00pm and 1:00pm - 5:00pm, and Friday from 8:00am - 12:00pm. We are here to answer the phone on Fridays, but Dr. Funicella only sees patients from Monday to Thursday. We are closed on most holidays; please call us for details.

What is your cancellation policy?

For the benefit of other patients, we request at least 24 hours notice for all cancellations and rescheduled appointments. Please call us as soon as you know of any changes to your scheduled appointment.

Do you offer appointment reminders?

Yes. Please request a reminder when scheduling your appointment, and we will call you the day before your appointment (or on Thursday for Monday appointments).

What should I do if I need a refill on a prescription?

Even if your prescription says there are no refills remaining, call your pharmacy and request a refill — they will fax us the information necessary to process your request.

What forms of health insurance do you accept?

Dr. Funicella is a participating provider for the following insurance companies:

  • Accountable Health Plan/Interplan Health Group (PPO)
  • Aetna (PPO)
  • Averde Health (PPO)
  • Beech Street, a Viant Network (PPO/PPO Next)
  • Blue Cross (PPO)
  • CIGNA (PPO & Open Access Plus)
  • FirstCare (PPO)
  • First Health (PPO Leased & Direct Business)
  • Galaxy Health Network (PPO)
  • Great-West Healthcare
  • Health Payors Organization (PPO)
  • HealthSmart Preferred Care (PPO) and HealthSmart Accel (PPO)
  • Integrated Medical Systems (PPO)
  • Medicare
  • MEGA Life, Student Insurance Division (PPO)
  • Seton Health Plan (Expanded EPN and EPN)
  • United Healthcare
  • USA Managed Care Organization (PPO)

If you are on one of these plans, we will file insurance claims on your behalf. If you have insurance through another company, you can still see Dr. Funicella out-of-network and pay for services at the time of your appointment. We will provide you with a receipt so that you can file a claim with your insurance company. Self-pay patients are also always welcome.

How long have you been in business?

Dr. Funicella has been in private practice since 1983.

What are Dr. Funicella's professional credentials?

These are listed in Dr. Funicella's biography.

When do I need to arrive for my appointment?

If you are a new patient, please plan to be here at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time so that we can gather all necessary information for your patient file. We encourage you to download and complete these forms prior to your visit.

If you are an established patient, please allow 10 minutes before your appointment time as we may need to ask for some information to update your medical records.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please be sure to bring:

  • your insurance card;
  • a photo ID such as your driver's license;
  • and, your checkbook or cash (we do not accept credit or debit cards) to cover any charges that you may incur.

We will also need to collect basic demographic information, information about your medical history, and a list of current medications (prescription and over-the-counter).

We encourage new patients to download and complete these forms prior to your appointment. This ensures that you are aware of all information that we need in advance of your visit.

What is the parking situation like at your office?

We are the only occupied office suite in our building, so there is plenty of adjacent parking throughout the day. We also have parking for the disabled and an access ramp directly in front of our office.
