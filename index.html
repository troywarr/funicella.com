Welcome to Our Website

Dr. Toni Funicella, M.D. has been proudly serving the greater Austin and central Texas area since 1983. Through her friendly, cheerful attitude and numerous professional credentials, she has earned a very good reputation among patients and medical peers alike.

Dr. Funicella's practice is primarily medical dermatology. She has extensive experience in treating skin cancers, pre-skin cancers, acne, hair loss, nail fungus, eczema/atopic dermatitis, contact dermatitis, bacterial skin infections, age-related skin changes, herpes simplex, psoriasis, rosacea, tinea versicolor and numerous other skin conditions.

This website is a resource for patients to learn more about Dr. Funicella's practice and procedures, and find useful information such as contact details and driving directions.

Here is a brief summary of what is available on each page:

  • About Us: Dr. Funicella's biography and basic information about our office
  • Location: office address, driving directions, and a map
  • F.A.Q. (Frequently Asked Questions): accepted insurance plans, what to bring to your appointment, and much more
  • Links: a collection of links to affiliated and/or related businesses and organizations in the field of dermatology
  • Contact Us: information to get in touch with our office staff

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about Toni Funicella, M.D. She looks forward to serving you and meeting your dermatological needs.
