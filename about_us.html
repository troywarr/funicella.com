About Us

Toni Funicella, M.D.

Dr. Funicella is board certified in both dermatology and internal medicine and has been in the private practice of clinical dermatology for over 20 years. She received her M.D. degree from The University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston, TX. Her dermatology training was at Baylor College of Medicine in Houston, TX.

Prior to her dermatology residency, Dr. Funicella trained in internal medicine/hematology at The University of Texas Medical School in Houston where she was on the Faculty of Medicine for several years.

Dr. Funicella's practice is primarily medical dermatology. She has extensive experience in treating skin cancers, pre-skin cancers, acne, hair loss, nail fungus, eczema/atopic dermatitis, contact dermatitis, bacterial skin infections, age-related skin changes, herpes simplex, psoriasis, rosacea, tinea versicolor and numerous other skin conditions.

Dr. Funicella's professional affiliations include the American Academy of Dermatology, Texas Dermatological Society, Travis County Medical Society, Texas Medical Association, Austin Dermatological Society, American Medical Association, Society for Pediatric Dermatology and the Women's Dermatologic Society.

She looks forward to serving you and meeting your dermatological needs.

Our Office

Dr. Funicella's office is located in the Lake Creek Office Park near the intersection of Research Blvd. (Hwy-183) and Lake Creek Pkwy. (driving directions and a printable map are available). It is a single-story building, and is the only office located in Building P (suite P-4). There is a ramp located near our front door to aid those with impaired mobility. Ample parking, including parking spaces for the disabled, make getting to and from your appointment a breeze!

Our friendly and helpful staff are here for you. If you have any questions, please contact us during our office hours, and we will be happy to assist you!
